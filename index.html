About us

Hill
focused

Our mission is to make life easier for Hill staff. We interviewed countless staff to understand their biggest pain points, and then designed software to help.

Before the CRMs (Constituent Response Managers), LCs used to have to deal with a bucket of physical mail. Right now, the rest of the staff use basic technologies that have been around for decades, which is not that much better, technologically, than having a legislative bucket at your feet.

MIO is going to give you the tools you never knew you couldn't live without. And we will continue building new solutions for hill staff over the long term.

Keep Organized and Coordinated!

The district and DC staff are working on a project, creating huge email chains, version issues with documents - leaving some staffers feeling overwhelmed and others feeling out of the loop.

MIO will allow you to collaborate, store documents in one place, and edit together in real time, without email!



The office needs to sign onto a letter quickly, but the approval request gets lost in someone’s email inbox.

MIO gives you an approval process that is automated and allows you to see exactly what's going on;   all while creating your office’s searchable database. This lets you understand your decisions and actions, past and present, making life easier when the same issue comes around the following Congress.

Automatically Keep a Searchable Record!

Your key staffer with all the institutional knowledge (and unique filing system) has left and now you need to get your new hire up to speed.

By keeping everything organized and easily searchable, the baton pass between staffers has never been easier. MIO will automatically create a searchable history of every vote you have taken, letter you signed onto, caucus you joined, bill you cosponsored, and more.


Your boss comes to you and says “I have a meeting with XXX in 30 minutes, tell me everything I have done in their issue area”

With MIO’s searchable database you will have an answer in seconds.

Collaborate Seamlessly

You are working with another office and outside stakeholders, but have to keep pinging the other office to get the latest version - and the stakeholders will not leave you alone. MIO will keep other offices, stakeholders, and outside groups updated in real time.

MIO allows you to share documents so everyone can get the latest: from cosponsors, signers, bill text, DC text, and more -- without harassing your office.
